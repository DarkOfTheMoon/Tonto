Citing TONTO

It is important to always cite the author who first described the algorithm which is used by TONTO to do a particular calculation. References to algorithms used in TONTO are given in this manual, and may be presented as output from some calculations.

You should cite TONTO if you are writing a paper which has modified parts of TONTO, and deals with aspects of efficiency or implementation, or if you are writing a paper about the construction of large computational chemistry programs, since TONTO is probably the only example of an object oriented Fortran computational chemistry system in existence.
