Philosophical motivation ...

It is our view that the great advances in computer software that have been made over the last decades are due largely to the free exchange of ideas and information, including the availability of good free software. It is also our belief that the lack of cooperation between researchers in the field of quantum chemistry has had a deleterious effect on the important role that quantum chemistry should be taking in chemistry, physics and engineering.

We hope that if you are a user and programmer, you will consider developing your ideas within this framework and that you will share your work. Hopefully, the code will evolve and be corrected by the user community.

Suggestions and comments will always be appreciated!
