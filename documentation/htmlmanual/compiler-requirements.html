Compiler requirements for TONTO

The set of compilers and operating systems on which TONTO is known to work:

  • Compaq f95 ≥ 5.5 under Tru64

  • Fujitsu frtvpp under UXP5

  • Fujitsu frtvpp cross compiler under Solaris

  • IBM xlf90 under AIX

  • Intel ifc 7.1 under Linux i386, build ≥ 20030507Z

  • Intel ifort 8.0 under Linux, build 20040412Z

  • Lahey-Fujitsu lf95 V6.0, V6.1 under Linux i386

  • Lahey-Fujitsu lf95 V5.7 under MS Windows

  • Silicon Graphics MIPSPRO under Irix64

  • NAG f95 V4.2 ≥ 538, V5.0 under Linux i386

  • Sun Forte V6, V7.0 under Solaris

The set of compilers and operating systems on which TONTO is known to fail:

  • Absoft Pro Fortran v8.0 for Linux i386

  • Compaq f95 < 5.5 under Tru64

  • DEC f95 under OSF1

  • Fujitsu f95 compiler under Linux i386

  • Intel ifc 5.0, 6.0, 7.0, 7.1 build < 20030507Z, under Linux i386

  • Intel ifort 8.0 under Linux i386, early builds

  • NAG f95 V4.2 < 538 under Linux i386

  • Portland Group V4.0 for Linux i386

To compile TONTO on MS Windows, you will need the standard unix utilities (available for free from Cygwin) in addition to those listed in the section called “System requirements for TONTO”.

Some compilers may need upgrading or require special settings. Have a look at section the section called “Known problems installing TONTO” for compiler specific issues.
